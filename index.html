Privacy Policy

Babblebook stores everything you record — audio, cover photos, the names you assign to recordings, and your child's name and birthday — on your device only. The developer does not collect, receive, or have access to your data.

The microphone, camera, and photo library are only accessed when you initiate a feature that needs them. The app does not run in the background.

There are no analytics, no trackers, no advertising, and no third-party services. The app does not open network connections.

In-app purchases are handled entirely by Apple via the App Store. The developer does not collect or store any payment information.

Apple may collect certain usage and diagnostic data through the App Store and iOS platform. This is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy at apple.com/privacy.
